Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Barry Counter ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our iOS application ("Barry Counter" or the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Game Center

Barry Counter integrates with Apple Game Center for social features. When you sign in with Game Center, we receive:

  • Game Center Player ID: A unique identifier assigned by Apple
  • Display Name: Your Game Center username
  • Profile Photo: Your Game Center profile image (automatically synced)
  • Friends List: Your Game Center friends who also use Barry Counter

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:

  • Counter entries and statistics
  • Scores and achievements
  • App interaction data (features used, session duration)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • IP address (for security and regional functionality)

1.3 Location Data

If you grant permission, we may collect your location to associate it with your counter entries. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate Barry Counter. Each service may collect data as described below:

2.1 Google Firebase

We use Google Firebase, a platform provided by Google LLC, for various backend services. Firebase is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. The Firebase services we use include:

Service Purpose Data Collected
Firebase Authentication User account management linked to Game Center User ID, authentication tokens
Cloud Firestore Store user profiles, scores, and friends data User data, scores, social connections
Firebase Storage Store profile images from Game Center Profile photos
Cloud Functions Server-side processing for features like friend management Data processed as needed for functionality
Firebase Analytics Understand app usage and improve the Service User identifier (Game Center ID), usage events
Remote Config Manage feature availability No personal data collected

For more information about how Google handles data, please see Firebase Privacy Information and Google's Privacy Policy.

2.2 Apple Game Center

We use Apple Game Center for leaderboards, achievements, and social features. Apple processes this data according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: Enable counter functionality, display statistics, and sync data across sessions
  • Social Features: Show leaderboards, friend comparisons, and achievements
  • Personalization: Remember your preferences, selected brands, and settings
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and user experience
  • Security: Protect against unauthorized access and ensure service integrity
  • Communication: Send notifications about your counter (if enabled) and important updates

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Other Users: Your display name, profile photo, and scores may be visible to your Game Center friends who use Barry Counter
  • Service Providers: With Google Firebase and Apple as described above
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States where Google's servers are located.

For EU/EEA Users: Google has implemented Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework to ensure adequate protection for data transferred outside the European Economic Area.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Counter Entries: Retained until you delete them or your account
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 14 months (Firebase Analytics default)
  • Profile Images: Retained until updated or account deleted

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored by the Service
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

7.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Analytics: You can limit ad tracking and analytics collection in your iOS device settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Location: Disable location access in Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Barry Counter
  • Notifications: Manage notification preferences in your device settings

7.3 Additional Rights for EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights including:

  • Right to object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through Firebase and Game Center
  • Access controls and authentication for backend systems

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Barry Counter is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Providing notice within the app for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
